Biology Review Flashcards

What is mitosis?
Cell division that creates two identical daughter cells.
What is DNA?
The molecule that carries genetic information.
Function of ribosomes?
They synthesize proteins in the cell.
Photosynthesis occurs in?
The chloroplasts of plant cells.
What is osmosis?
Diffusion of water through a semi-permeable membrane.
What is a gene?
A segment of DNA that codes for a trait.
Function of the nucleus?
It controls cell activities and stores DNA.
Function of chlorophyll?
It absorbs light for photosynthesis.
